```
Vantag ile her harcamanin gercek maliyetini ogren!

Hic dusundun mu, bir kahve icin kac saat calisiyorsun? iPhone almak icin kac gun mesai yapman gerekiyor? Vantag, harcamalarini calisma saatine cevirerek paranin gercek degerini anlamani saglar.

NASIL CALISIR?
1. Gelirini ve calisma saatlerini gir
2. Harcamani ekle
3. Kac saat calistiginizi gorun
4. Akillica karar verin: "Aldim", "Dusunuyorum" veya "Vazgectim"

TEMEL OZELLIKLER

Calisma Saati Hesaplama
Her harcamanin kac saatlik emege karsilik geldigini aninda gorun. 500 TL = 12 saat calisma gibi somut rakamlar.

AI Finans Asistani
Yapay zeka destekli asistan ile harcamalarinizi analiz edin. "Bu ay nereye harcadim?" diye sorun, detayli cevap alin.

Hayallerim (Tasarruf Hedefleri)
Telefon, tatil, araba... Hedeflerinizi belirleyin, ilerlemenizi takip edin. Vazgectiginiz harcamalar otomatik birikir.

Abonelik Takibi
Netflix, Spotify, spor salonu... Tum aboneliklerinizi tek yerde gorun. Yenileme tarihleri yaklasinca bildirim alin.

Sesli Giris
"Kahveye 80 lira verdim" deyin, otomatik kayit edilsin. Hizli ve kolay.

Detayli Raporlar
Haftalik, aylik, yillik raporlar. Kategori bazli analiz. Nereye para akiyor, aninda gorun.

Basari Rozetleri
Tasarruf hedeflerine ulasinca rozetler kazanin. Finansal aliskanliklari oyunlastirin.

NEDEN VANTAG?

"Zamanin = Paran" felsefesiyle calisiriz. Bir harcamayi TL olarak degil, hayatinizdan ne kadar zaman aldigini gosteriyoruz. Bu bakis acisi, daha bilinçli kararlar vermenizi saglar.

UCRETSIZ OZELLIKLER
- Sinirsiz harcama kaydi
- Temel raporlar
- 1 tasarruf hedefi
- 4 hazir AI soru butonu
- 5 gunluk AI sohbet

PRO OZELLIKLER
- Sinirsiz AI sohbet
- Sinirsiz tasarruf hedefi
- Tum raporlar (haftalik, aylik, yillik)
- Excel ve PDF export
- 5 para birimi destegi
- Banka ekstresi iceri aktarma

GIZLILIK
Verileriniz guvenli. Firebase ile sifrelenmis depolama. Finansal verileriniz sadece sizin.

DESTEK
Sorulariniz icin: vantagfinance@gmail.com

Finansal ozgurluge giden yol, bilinçli harcamalardan gecer. Vantag ile bugunden baslayin!

### Full Description (4000 characters max)

```
Discover the true cost of every purchase with Vantag!

Have you ever wondered how many hours you work for a coffee? How many days of work does an iPhone cost? Vantag converts your expenses into work hours, helping you understand the real value of money.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Enter your income and work hours
2. Add your expense
3. See how many hours you worked for it
4. Make smart decisions: "Bought", "Thinking", or "Passed"

KEY FEATURES

Work Hours Calculator
Instantly see how many hours of work each expense represents. 50 USD = 3 hours of work - concrete numbers that matter.

AI Finance Assistant
Analyze your spending with our AI-powered assistant. Ask "Where did I spend this month?" and get detailed insights.

Dreams (Savings Goals)
Phone, vacation, car... Set your goals and track progress. Money you decide not to spend automatically goes to savings.

Subscription Tracker
Netflix, Spotify, gym... See all subscriptions in one place. Get reminders before renewal dates.

Voice Input
Say "I spent 10 dollars on coffee" and it's recorded automatically. Quick and easy.

Detailed Reports
Weekly, monthly, yearly reports. Category analysis. See where your money flows instantly.

Achievement Badges
Earn badges when you reach savings goals. Gamify your financial habits.

WHY VANTAG?

We work with the "Time = Money" philosophy. We show you not just the price, but how much of your life each purchase costs. This perspective helps you make more conscious decisions.

FREE FEATURES
- Unlimited expense tracking
- Basic reports
- 1 savings goal
- 4 preset AI question buttons
- 5 daily AI chats

PRO FEATURES
- Unlimited AI chat
- Unlimited savings goals
- All reports (weekly, monthly, yearly)
- Excel and PDF export
- 5 currency support
- Bank statement import

PRIVACY
Your data is secure. Encrypted storage with Firebase. Your financial data stays yours.

SUPPORT
Questions? vantagfinance@gmail.com

The path to financial freedom starts with conscious spending. Start today with Vantag!
