Privacy Policy
Last Updated: January 18, 2026
Introduction
Vantag ("App", "we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use our app, how it is used, and how it is protected.
By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Information You Provide
When using the App, you enter the following information:

Income information: Your monthly income and income sources
Work information: Daily working hours, weekly working days
Expense records: Amounts, categories, descriptions, and dates
Subscription information: Subscription names, amounts, and renewal dates
Profile photo: Optionally added by you

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

Device information: Device model, operating system version
App usage data: Open count, features used
Crash reports: App crash information (via Crashlytics)
Voice data: Voice commands are converted to text on your device, no audio is stored
Receipt images: Processed on device (Google ML Kit), images are not sent to servers

1.3 Google Sign-In
If you sign in with your Google account:

Google account ID
Email address
Display name
Profile photo URL


2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:

Provide core app functionality (expense tracking, calculations)
Sync your data across devices
Improve the app and fix bugs
Enhance user experience

We never:

Sell your information to third parties
Use your information for advertising
Share your information without your consent


3. Data Storage
3.1 Local Storage
The following data is stored only on your device:

Income and work information
Subscription records
App settings
Achievement and streak records

3.2 Cloud Storage
The following data is securely stored on Google Firebase infrastructure:

User credentials (if signed in with Google)
Expense records

Firebase operates according to Google's security standards, and your data is stored encrypted.

4. Data Security
To protect your data:

We use SSL/TLS encryption
We implement Firebase security rules
Local data is protected by your device's security mechanisms
Only you can access your data


5. Data Sharing
We do not share your data except in the following cases:

Legal requirement: When required by court order or legal process
Service providers: For Firebase (Google) infrastructure services

### Artificial Intelligence Services
The app uses the following AI services:
- **OpenAI (GPT-4o)**: For processing voice commands and AI chat
- **Google Gemini**: For AI memory and recommendation system

Data sent to these services:
- Is processed anonymously (no personal information is sent)

- Data is NOT used for AI training
