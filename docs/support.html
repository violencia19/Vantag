Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add an expense?
Tap the + button on the home screen. Enter the amount, select a category, and add an optional note. Vantag will automatically convert the cost into work hours based on your salary settings.
How do subscriptions work?
Go to the subscriptions section from the home screen. You can add recurring expenses (Netflix, Spotify, etc.) with their billing cycle. Vantag tracks them separately and shows you the total monthly cost in work hours.
How do I set my salary?
Open Settings and tap Salary. Enter your monthly or yearly salary. This is used to calculate how many hours you need to work to cover each expense.
How do I delete my account?
Open Settings > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data from the app. Since Vantag stores data locally on your device, uninstalling the app also removes all data.
How does voice input work?
Tap the microphone icon on the expense screen and say the amount and category naturally, like "50 lira for coffee." Vantag will parse your speech and fill in the expense details automatically.
Is my data private?
Yes. All your financial data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit your expense or salary information to any server. Read our full Privacy Policy.
